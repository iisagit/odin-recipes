Sandwich recipe

Description

Club Sandwich as the name suggests is loaded with veggies. From bell peppers to lettuce, it has all your favorite vegetables stuffed inside toasted bread slices. The filling includes mayo and crispy potato slices. This meal is enough to keep you full for a longer duration and makes for a classic breakfast. On days, when you don't want to eat basic sandwich or eggs, Club Sandwich would be a life-saver! Do try out this amazing recipe and let us know if you enjoyed making one!

Ingredients

  • 6 multi-grain bread
  • 3 cloves chopped garlic
  • 1/4 sliced,chopped red bell pepper
  • 1/4 chopped,sliced capsicum (green pepper)
  • 1 medium shredded carrot
